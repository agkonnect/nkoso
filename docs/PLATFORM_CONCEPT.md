# 🌍 NKOSO — *Grow Together*
### A Social Investment & Collaboration Platform for African Startups

> *"Nkoso"* means **growth** in Twi (Akan, Ghana)

---

## 🎯 Vision
Nkoso is a social-first platform where Ghanaian (and broader African) entrepreneurs
list their businesses or ideas, and a community of supporters can engage in three
powerful ways — volunteer their skills, invest money, or buy equity shares.

Think: **AngelList meets TikTok meets Kickstarter**, built for the African market.

---

## 👥 User Types

| User Type     | Who They Are                          | What They Do                                      |
|---------------|---------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| 🧠 Founders   | Startups, SMEs, idea-stage founders   | List businesses, pitch ideas, post updates        |
| 🙌 Volunteers | Students, professionals, creatives    | Offer skills — design, coding, marketing, legal   |
| 💰 Investors  | Individuals, angels, diaspora         | Invest money for equity or returns                |
| 🛒 Buyers     | Community members                     | Buy micro-shares / fractional equity              |
| 🏢 Sponsors   | Corporates, NGOs, accelerators        | Fund challenges, sponsor startups, offer grants   |

---

## 🏗️ Core Features

### 1. Business Listings (The "Pitch Wall")
- Video pitch (30–90 sec)
- Business plan summary
- Funding goals & equity offered
- Skills needed for volunteers
- Verified founder profiles

### 2. Three Ways to Support
- **Volunteer**: Apply skills, earn reputation score, convert hours to equity
- **Invest**: Mobile Money investment, escrow-protected, digital certificates
- **Buy Shares**: Micro-equity from GHS 10/share, blockchain-backed

### 3. Social Layer
- Scrollable pitch feed (TikTok-style)
- Reactions, comments, shares
- Founder Diaries (progress stories)
- Leaderboards & discovery
- Follow businesses

### 4. Trust & Safety
- Ghana Card / Passport ID verification
- Business registration verification
- Escrow for investor funds
- Review & rating system
- Dispute resolution

### 5. Payments
- MTN Mobile Money
- Vodafone Cash / AirtelTigo
- Bank Transfer / Card
- Diaspora transfers (USD/GBP/EUR → GHS)
- Via: Paystack / Hubtel

## 💰 Monetization

| Stream                 | How It Works                                         |
|------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Transaction Fee        | 2–5% on investments and share purchases              |
| Premium Listings       | Founders pay to boost/feature listing                |
| Verified Badge         | Monthly subscription for trust badges                |
| Accelerator Partners   | Revenue share on sourced deals                       |
| Corporate Sponsorships | Companies sponsor pitch competitions                 |
| Data Insights (opt-in) | Aggregate reports sold to VCs, NGOs, govt            |

## 🗺️ Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Launch (Months 1–6)
- Business listings + pitch videos
- Volunteer matching
- Basic investment (equity crowdfunding)
- Mobile Money payments
- Social feed + reactions + comments

### Phase 2 — Growth (Months 6–18)
- Micro-share marketplace
- Secondary share trading
- Blockchain shareholder certificates (Polygon)
- Diaspora investment flow
- Accelerator & corporate partnerships

### Phase 3 — Scale (18+ Months)
- Expand to Nigeria, Kenya, Côte d'Ivoire
- SEC Ghana crowdfunding license
- Nkoso Fund (platform-managed investment fund)
- B2B API for banks and financial institutions
